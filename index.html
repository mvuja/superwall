$BRCK Token

The $BRCK token is built on the ERC-20 standards, and it will be the official token of the Superwall ecosystem. The SuperWall experts are integrating the benefits given by the ethereum blockchain and DeFi to create $BRCK token that will allow users to hold, transact, invest, and bid on contents (NFTs) of Supers (influencers) without external interference. The $BRCK token will serve as one of the underlying currencies for products and services built on the Superwall platform.

As the Superwall ecosystem grows, the $BRCK token will also grow in value. Holders of the $BRCK will be able to experience far less unpredictable fluctuations that occur within the cryptocurrency market, as the Superwall platform is backed by an ecosystem whose survival is not only dependent on the prevailing market price of cryptocurrency.
